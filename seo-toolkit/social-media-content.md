### 模板3：行业知识帖（提升专业形象）
```
📊 What affects the price of custom printed ribbons?

Most buyers only compare the per-meter price. But the real cost drivers are:

1️⃣ Design complexity
Simple logos = lower setup cost. Full-bleed photographic prints = higher.

2️⃣ Color count
Each color = 1 printing pass. A 3-color design vs 7-color design = big price difference.

3️⃣ Width & material
Wider ribbons = more material = higher cost.
Satin vs grosgrain = different material prices.

4️⃣ Order quantity
The more you order, the lower the per-meter cost (setup fee spreads out).

💡 Our tip: Keep your design to 3 colors max for the best price-to-quality ratio.

What would you like to know more about? Comment below 👇
```

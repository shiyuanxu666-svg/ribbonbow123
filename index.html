Frequently Asked Questions

What is your minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

For custom printed ribbons: 1000 meters per color. For stock products: no MOQ. Sample orders are welcome.

How long is your production lead time?

Sample: 3-7 days. Mass production: 15-25 days depending on order quantity and complexity.

Do you provide free samples?

Yes, we provide free stock samples. Custom samples with printing fee may apply.

What payment methods do you accept?

T/T, L/C, PayPal, Western Union. For first-time clients, we usually require 30% deposit, 70% before shipment.

Can you ship to our freight forwarder in China?

Yes, we can ship to any freight forwarder or warehouse in China. We also arrange shipping worldwide.

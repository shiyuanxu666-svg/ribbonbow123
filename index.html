About Smith Ribbon

Your Trusted Ribbon & Bow Manufacturer in China

Your OEM & Private Label Ribbon Partner in China

Founded in 2004, Smith Ribbon is a professional OEM and private label ribbon manufacturer in Xiamen, Fujian Province, China. With over 20 years of industry experience, we specialize in providing customized ribbon solutions for global brands, retailers, beauty companies, and gift importers.

Our company specializes in manufacturing a wide range of ribbon products including printed ribbons, satin ribbons, jacquard ribbons, grosgrain ribbons, organza ribbons, velvet ribbons, and custom-made bows. We serve clients across various industries including gift packaging, fashion accessories, wedding decorations, holiday crafts, and promotional products.

At Smith Ribbon, we are committed to providing high-quality products at competitive factory prices. Our dedicated team of 200+ employees works diligently to ensure every product meets international quality standards. We welcome OEM and ODM orders, and offer customized solutions to meet your specific requirements.

Our mission is to help customers succeed by providing them with high-quality, innovative ribbon products that enhance their product packaging and brand value. We look forward to building long-term business relationships with clients worldwide.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is your minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

For custom printed ribbons with logo: 1,000 meters per color. For stock products: no minimum order quantity (MOQ). Sample orders are always welcome. We also offer small trial orders for new clients.

How long is your production lead time?

Sample production: 3-7 days. Mass production: 15-25 days depending on order quantity and product complexity. For large orders, we provide production schedule updates every 3-5 days.

Do you provide free samples?

Yes, we provide free stock samples (1-2 meters each). For custom samples with logo printing, a sample fee may apply, which will be refunded after mass production order is confirmed.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept T/T (Telegraphic Transfer), L/C (Letter of Credit), PayPal, Western Union, and Alibaba Trade Assurance. For first-time clients, we usually require 30% deposit, 70% before shipment.

Can you ship to our freight forwarder in China?

Absolutely! We can ship to any freight forwarder, warehouse, or port in China. We also offer complete shipping services worldwide through our trusted logistics partners.

Do you offer custom packaging?

Yes, we offer various packaging options including poly bags, OPP bags, gift boxes, and custom printed packaging. Please inform us of your packaging requirements when requesting a quote.

What are your main export markets?

We export to over 50 countries including USA, UK, Germany, France, Italy, Spain, Japan, Australia, Canada, Brazil, and many more. Major markets are North America and Western Europe.

What Is Grosgrain Ribbon?

Double-Sided Grosgrain Ribbon

Smooth and lustrous on both sides with the characteristic ribbed texture. Ideal for applications where both sides of the ribbon will be visible — bows, loops, and decorative use.

Single-Sided Grosgrain Ribbon

Grosgrain texture on the face side, plain backing on the reverse. More economical for high-volume applications where only one side is visible — printed ribbons, tags, and industrial use.

Custom Printed Grosgrain Ribbon

Grosgrain is the number one material for printed brand ribbons — the ribbed texture holds ink exceptionally well, producing durable, wash-resistant prints. Screen printing is the most popular method. MOQ 1000m.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is grosgrain ribbon and what makes it different?

Grosgrain is a closely woven ribbon with a distinctive ribbed texture — visible crosswise ridges. It is more durable and structured than satin ribbon, making it the number one choice for gift bows, clothing trims, and printed brand ribbons.

Q: What is the difference between grosgrain and twill ribbon?

Grosgrain has a pronounced ribbed texture running across the width of the ribbon. Twill has diagonal parallel ridges like denim. Grosgrain is smoother and more formal. Twill is more casual and durable for heavy-duty use.

Q: Can grosgrain ribbons be printed on?

Yes. Grosgrain is one of the best ribbon materials for printing because the textured surface holds ink well, producing durable, wash-resistant prints. Screen printing on grosgrain is our most popular custom product. MOQ 1000m.

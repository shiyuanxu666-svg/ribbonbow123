What Is Grosgrain Ribbon?

Grosgrain is a closely woven ribbon distinguished by its signature ribbed texture — fine ridges running across the width of the fabric. This gives grosgrain a structured, sophisticated appearance and makes it significantly more durable than plain weave ribbons. Grosgrain is one of the most versatile ribbon types in the world, used across fashion, packaging, crafts, and industrial applications.

Types of Grosgrain Ribbons We Offer

Double-Sided Grosgrain Ribbon

Smooth and lustrous on both sides with the characteristic ribbed texture. Ideal for applications where both sides of the ribbon will be visible — bows, loops, and decorative use.

Single-Sided Grosgrain Ribbon

Grosgrain texture on the face side, plain backing on the reverse. More economical for high-volume applications where only one side is visible — printed ribbons, tags, and industrial use.

Custom Printed Grosgrain Ribbon

Grosgrain is the #1 material for printed brand ribbons — the ribbed texture holds ink exceptionally well, producing durable, wash-resistant prints. Screen printing is the most popular method. MOQ 1,000m per design.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is grosgrain ribbon and what makes it different?

Grosgrain is a closely woven ribbon with a distinctive ribbed texture (visible crosswise ridges). It is more durable and structured than satin ribbon, making it the #1 choice for gift bows, clothing trims, and printed brand ribbons. The ribbed texture also holds ink better for printing.

Q: What is the difference between grosgrain and twill ribbon?

Grosgrain has a pronounced ribbed texture running across the width of the ribbon (narrow ridges). Twill has diagonal parallel ridges (like denim). Grosgrain is smoother and more formal; twill is more casual and durable for heavy-duty use.

Q: Can grosgrain ribbons be printed on?

Yes. Grosgrain is one of the best ribbon materials for printing because the textured surface holds ink well, producing durable, wash-resistant prints. Screen printing on grosgrain is our most popular custom product. MOQ: 1,000m.

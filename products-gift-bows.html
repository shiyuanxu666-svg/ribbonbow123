Bow Styles We Offer

We produce six primary bow categories. Each is available in multiple ribbon materials, widths, and colors to match your packaging or decor system:

🎀 Pull Bows

also known as Easy-Tie Bows — loops are pre-formed on a pull string. Simply pull both ends and the bow cinches tight in under 2 seconds. The #1 choice for high-volume gift packaging operations. Available in widths from 25mm to 75mm.

✋ Hand-Tied Bows

Classic, full-loop bows assembled by our skilled factory workers for a natural, artisanal appearance. Each loop is shaped individually. Ideal for luxury retail, wedding favors, and premium corporate gifts. Standard widths: 38mm, 50mm.

🌸 Corsage Bows / Boutonniere Loops

Compact, 3–5 loop bows typically in 10mm or 16mm width. Designed for corsages, boutonnieres, floral arrangements and small gift tags. Often offered in satin or grosgrain for a neat, tailored profile.

🎁 Multi-Loop Pull Bows

Extra-full pull bows with 6, 8, or 10 loops for oversized gift boxes, wine bottles, and promotional packaging. A single pull bow on a large present creates a statement look without the labor of manual tying.

🌿 Curly Ribbon Bows

A continuous curly ribbon pre-formed into a loose bow shape. Popular for holiday gift packaging, children's party favors, and decorative displays. The natural spring of the ribbon adds movement and whimsy.

🏛️ Fan Bows / Fan-Tail Bows

Wide, flat ribbon tails spread into a fan shape — popular for wedding centerpieces, bouquet wrapping, and formal event decor. Often made in satin or organza with 50mm–100mm widths.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is the difference between a pull bow and a hand-tied bow?

A: A pull bow has pre-formed loops attached to a pull string — pulling the string cinches the loops closed simultaneously. A hand-tied bow is assembled and knotted by hand, producing a more organic, slightly asymmetrical look. Pull bows are faster to apply; hand-tied bows are preferred for luxury presentations.

Q: Can I get bows in the same color as your flat ribbon rolls?

A: Yes. Our production team can dye both the ribbon used for bows and the flat ribbon rolls from the same dye lot, ensuring a perfect color match across all your packaging components.

Q: What is the minimum order for custom-colored bows?

A: Custom colors require a minimum of 500 pieces per color per bow style. Custom sizes or loop counts have an MOQ of 1,000 pieces.

Q: Can you add a logo or pattern to the bow tails?

A: Yes. We can produce jacquard-woven bows with your logo or brand pattern in the ribbon tails, or apply custom-printed ribbon to bows (MOQ: 2,000 pieces for custom printed ribbon).

Q: Do you offer seasonal (Christmas, Valentine's) bow collections?

A: We release seasonal collections 90 days before each major holiday, with popular styles kept in year-round stock. Contact us 60 days before your peak season to secure inventory.

Q: What attachment method do your bows use?

A: Standard bows have a self-adhesive dot on the back (peel-and-stick) and/or a small twist tie. We also offer bows with metal clips for mechanical attachment. Custom attachment options are available for bulk orders.

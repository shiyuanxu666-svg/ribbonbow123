Why Choose Ribbon Flowers?

Ribbon flowers add an unmistakable touch of elegance to any product or décor. Unlike fresh flowers, ribbon flowers are permanent — they don't wilt, don't need water, and last forever. They transform ordinary gift packaging into memorable unboxing experiences, and elevate wedding and home décor with lasting beauty.

RibbonBow has specialized in ribbon flower manufacturing since 2004. Our skilled craftsmen create ribbon flowers with precise folding and stitching techniques that give each flower a natural, lifelike appearance.

Flower Types We Offer

Ribbon Roses

The most classic ribbon flower — layered, ruffled petals arranged in a traditional rose shape. Available in both flat-backed (for attaching to boxes and cards) and stem styles (for bouquets and arrangements). Our satin ribbon roses are the #1 bestseller for wedding favor packaging.

Rosettes

Fan-shaped ribbon flowers with a center knot or button. Rosettes are one of the most versatile ribbon flowers — they can be used as lapel pins, gift box decorations, hat accessories, and bag charms. Also called "Chinese knot flowers" when made with folded ribbon in traditional techniques.

Ribbon Carnations

Full, ruffled ribbon flowers that closely resemble natural carnations. Available in a wider range of sizes than roses — from tiny 1cm accent carnations to large 15cm statement pieces.

Ribbon Peonies

Lush, full blooms that mimic the romantic fullness of natural peonies. Made with multiple layers of organza or satin ribbon for exceptional depth and texture. The premium choice for luxury wedding flowers and high-end gift packaging.

Materials & Colors

  • Satin Ribbon Flowers: Classic, smooth, lustrous finish. The most popular choice for weddings and gifts.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is the minimum order quantity for ribbon flowers?

Stock ribbon flowers: MOQ 50 pieces per style/color. Custom ribbon flowers (your color, size, or design): MOQ 200 pieces per specification. Sample orders of 10-20 pieces available for quality assessment.

Q: Can ribbon flowers be used for outdoor events?

Yes, but with considerations. Polyester satin and grosgrain ribbon flowers are weather-resistant and suitable for outdoor use. Velvet flowers are not recommended for prolonged outdoor exposure as the velvet texture can be affected by moisture. Organza flowers work outdoors but may need light protection from strong wind.

Q: Do you offer custom ribbon flower designs with our logo?

Yes. We can create custom ribbon flowers in your brand colors, with custom ribbon material, and custom packaging with your logo. MOQ for custom ribbon flowers: 200 pieces per design. Lead time: 20-30 days from design approval.

Q: Can I get ribbon flowers attached to hair clips or pins?

Absolutely. We offer ribbon flowers with pre-attached alligator clips, French barrettes, bobby pins, and hat brooch backs. MOQ for attached accessories: 100 pieces per style.

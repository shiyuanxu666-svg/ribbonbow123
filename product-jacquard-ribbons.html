What Makes Jacquard Ribbons Special

Jacquard ribbons are woven on specialized jacquard looms that control each warp thread individually, allowing for intricate, multi-dimensional patterns that are fully integrated into the fabric — not printed on the surface. The result: a ribbon with remarkable texture, durability, and a premium look that printed ribbons cannot match.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is the difference between jacquard ribbon and printed ribbon?

Jacquard ribbons have patterns woven directly into the fabric using a jacquard loom — the pattern is part of the ribbon structure, not surface-printed. This makes jacquard more durable, textured, and premium-feeling than printed ribbons. Jacquard is ideal for luxury branding; printed is more cost-effective for simple logos.

Q: What is the MOQ for custom jacquard patterns?

Custom jacquard patterns require a weaving mold/dye cast — MOQ 3,000m for custom patterns. Stock jacquard designs: MOQ 1,000m per color from existing patterns. Sample orders welcome.

Q: What widths are available?

Widths from 10mm to 150mm. Most common widths: 15mm, 20mm, 25mm, 30mm, 40mm, 50mm, 70mm, 100mm. Custom widths available with MOQ 3,000m.

Q: What are jacquard ribbons used for?

Luxury gift packaging, fashion apparel (belts, trim, labels), home décor, award ribbons, military insignia, scout and organization badges, and premium consumer product branding.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is the difference between jacquard ribbon and printed ribbon?

Jacquard ribbons have patterns woven directly into the fabric using a jacquard loom — the pattern is part of the ribbon structure, not surface-printed. This makes jacquard more durable, textured, and premium-feeling than printed ribbons.

Q: What is the MOQ for custom jacquard patterns?

Custom jacquard patterns require a weaving mold, MOQ 3000m for custom patterns. Stock jacquard designs MOQ 1000m per color from existing patterns.

Q: What widths are available?

Widths from 10mm to 150mm. Most common: 15mm, 20mm, 25mm, 30mm, 40mm, 50mm, 70mm, 100mm.

Q: What are jacquard ribbons used for?

Luxury gift packaging, fashion apparel belts, trim, labels, home decor, award ribbons, military insignia, scout and organization badges.

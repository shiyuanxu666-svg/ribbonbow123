Self-Adhesive Pull Bows

The #1 bestselling bow type for gift packaging. Simply peel off the backing and press onto any surface — no glue, no tape, no tools needed. The pull-string mechanism creates an instant voluminous bow when needed. Widely used in retail gift boxes, e-commerce packaging, and bakery boxes.

Pre-Tied Ribbon Bows

Already formed into a fixed knot shape. More durable than pull bows — the bow maintains its shape perfectly through shipping, handling, and storage. Ideal for bakery boxes, wine bags, mail-order subscription boxes, and food gift packaging.

Wired Gift Bows

Built-in wire edges allow bows to be shaped and reshaped for any occasion. The most versatile bow type — wired bows hold their shape for Christmas trees, wreaths, floral arrangements, and decorative displays. Popular for craft stores and home décor retailers.

Pull Ribbon (DIY Bow-Making)

Continuous rolls of ribbon with a pull-string mechanism — allows users to create custom-sized bows by cutting their own length. Popular for craft stores and DIY gift wrapping enthusiasts.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is the difference between a pull bow and a pre-tied bow?

A pull bow uses a pull-string mechanism — when pulled, the strings cinch the center creating an instant voluminous double-loop bow. A pre-tied bow is already knotted and formed into a fixed shape. Pull bows are faster to apply; pre-tied bows hold their shape better through shipping.

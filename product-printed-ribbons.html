Printing Methods We Offer

Screen Printing

The most cost-effective method for large orders. Ink is pushed through a mesh stencil screen onto the ribbon. Up to 12 spot colors with excellent color vibrancy and durability. Best for simple logos, text, and repeated patterns.

Digital Printing

Direct-to-ribbon digital printing — no screens or setup required. Perfect for complex designs, photographic images, gradients, and multi-color patterns. Ideal for short runs and sample orders.

Heat Transfer Printing

Designs are first printed on transfer paper then heat-pressed onto the ribbon. Delivers photo-quality results with excellent wash durability. Suitable for fine details and photographic-quality brand imagery.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is the minimum order quantity for custom printed ribbons?

Screen printed ribbons MOQ 1000m per color/design. Digital printing MOQ 500m. Heat transfer MOQ 1000m. Stock printed ribbons no MOQ.

Q: What printing methods do you offer?

Screen Printing — best for bulk orders and simple logos, most cost-effective at high volumes. Digital Printing — perfect for complex designs and photographs. Heat Transfer — photo-quality for intricate patterns and gradients.

Q: What materials can be printed on?

Polyester Satin (most popular), Grosgrain, Polyester Taffeta, Velvet, and Organza. Each material offers different visual effects and durability.

Q: What file formats do you need for custom printing?

AI, EPS, PDF, or high-resolution JPEG/PNG 300 DPI minimum. Our design team can help finalize artwork at no extra charge.

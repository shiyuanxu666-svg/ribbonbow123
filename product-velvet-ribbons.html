Velvet Ribbon Types

Velvet Satin Ribbon

Smooth velvet on one side with a satin back. Combines the luxury of velvet with the subtle sheen of satin. The most versatile velvet option — ideal for gift wrapping, bows, and decorative applications.

Velvet Grosgrain Ribbon

Velvet weave with a distinctive ribbed texture on the back. The ribs provide excellent grip for bow-making and knot-tying. Ideal for hair bows, gift bows, and applications where the ribbon needs to hold its shape.

Double-Face Velvet

Velvet on both sides — truly luxurious when draped or folded. Premium choice for luxury fashion packaging, jewelry boxes, and high-end wedding stationery.

How to Care for Velvet Ribbon

To maintain the luxurious texture and rich color of velvet ribbon:

  • Storage: Keep rolled or loosely folded in a dust-free environment. Avoid crushing the velvet pile.
  • Cleaning: Gently spot-clean with a damp cloth. For hand-washing: cold water, mild detergent, no rubbing. Air dry away from heat.
  • Cutting: Use sharp scissors for clean cuts. Seal cut edges with a lighter or hot knife to prevent fraying.
  • Avoid: Direct sunlight (fades colors over time), high heat, bleach, and machine washing.

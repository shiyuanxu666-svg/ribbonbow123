What Makes Wired Ribbon Special?

Wired ribbon has thin metal wires running along both edges (single wire) or along both edges plus through the center (double wire). These wires allow the ribbon to be shaped, bent, and twisted into any form — and crucially, the ribbon holds its shape once formed. This makes wired ribbon essential for three key applications: Christmas decorations, floral arrangements, and craft projects.

Wired Ribbon Types

Single Wire Edge Ribbon

One thin wire runs along each edge of the ribbon. The wire allows the outer edges to be shaped and curled while the center remains flat. The most versatile wired ribbon type — suitable for most bow-making and craft applications.

Double Wire Edge Ribbon

Wires run along both edges AND a wire runs through the center of the ribbon. This creates maximum control over shaping — the entire ribbon can be formed into complex curves, loops, and spirals. Premium choice for wedding floral work and complex decorative installations.

Chenille Wire (Pipe Cleaner Wire)

Full-width flexible wire running through the center of the ribbon body — allows the ribbon to be shaped in any direction. Popular for craft projects, children's decorations, and DIY floral arrangements.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is the difference between single wire and double wire edge ribbon?

Single wire edge: one wire runs along each outer edge — allows outer edges to be shaped while center stays flat. Double wire edge: wires along both edges plus a center wire — allows the entire ribbon to be shaped in any direction. Double wire is preferred for complex floral work and wedding décor.

Q: Can wired ribbon be washed?

Hand-washing wired ribbon is not recommended — water can cause the wire to rust and damage the ribbon. For outdoor applications, use polyester wired ribbons with plastic-coated wire. For floral applications, remove wired ribbon before washing any decorated items.

Q: What is the MOQ for wired ribbon?

Stock wired ribbon: MOQ 200m per color. Custom wired ribbon (special colors or widths): MOQ 500m. Sample orders of 30-50m available for quality assessment.

Q: Can wired ribbon be used for outdoor Christmas decorations?

Yes. Polyester wired ribbon with plastic-coated wire is weather-resistant and suitable for outdoor use. We recommend storing outdoor wired decorations in a dry location between seasons to maximize ribbon lifespan.

Types of Satin Ribbons We Offer

Double-Sided Satin Ribbon

Our flagship product. Smooth and lustrous on both sides — the gold standard for gift wrapping, bows, floral arrangements, and fashion accessories. The most popular ribbon type for premium packaging and craft applications worldwide.

Single-Sided Satin Ribbon

Satin finish on the front, matte backing on the reverse. More cost-effective for high-volume applications. Ideal for printing, industrial use, and where only one side is visible.

Custom Printed Satin Ribbon

Your logo, pattern, or message custom-printed on satin ribbon. Screen printing, digital printing, and heat transfer options. Perfect for brand packaging, promotional events, and corporate gifts.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is the difference between single-sided and double-sided satin ribbon?

Double-sided satin ribbon has a satin finish on both sides — ideal for bows and visible applications. Single-sided satin has satin on the front and a matte finish on the back, making it more suitable for printing and cost-sensitive applications.

Q: What is the MOQ for custom satin ribbons?

Stock satin ribbons: MOQ 500m per color. Custom colors: MOQ 1,000m. Custom printed satin: MOQ 1,000m. Sample rolls of 10-50m available for quality inspection.

Q: What widths and colors are available?

Widths: 3mm to 100mm (most popular: 10mm, 15mm, 25mm, 38mm, 50mm). Stock colors: 200+ colors including Pantone-matched custom colors. Common roll lengths: 50 yards, 100 yards, or 1,000 yards.

Q: Are your satin ribbons OEKO-TEX certified?

Yes. All our satin ribbons are OEKO-TEX Standard 100 certified — safe for direct contact with skin, children's products, and food packaging. Certificate available upon request.

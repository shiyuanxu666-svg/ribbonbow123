Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is the difference between single-sided and double-sided satin ribbon?

Double-sided satin ribbon has a satin finish on both sides — ideal for bows and visible applications. Single-sided has satin on the front and matte on the back, more suitable for printing.

Q: What is the MOQ for custom satin ribbons?

Stock satin ribbons MOQ 500m per color. Custom colors MOQ 1000m. Custom printed satin MOQ 1000m. Sample rolls of 10-50m available.

Q: What widths and colors are available?

Widths 3mm to 100mm, most popular 10mm, 15mm, 25mm, 38mm, 50mm. Stock colors 200+ colors including Pantone-matched custom colors.

Q: Are your satin ribbons OEKO-TEX certified?

Yes. All our satin ribbons are OEKO-TEX Standard 100 certified — safe for direct contact with skin, children products, and food packaging.

3. Hidden Cost Factors

  • Sample cost: China $30-80 / Vietnam $50-100 / India $40-90 / Indonesia $50-110
  • Tooling/Mold setup: $150-600 one-time per design/color
  • Color matching: $50-120 per color, additional lead time 3-5 days
  • Certification testing: OEKO-TEX or BSCI audit adds $200-600 to first order
  • Sea freight (China to EU): $0.12-0.25/kg; a 1,000m standard satin order weighs ~4-6kg

4. 2026 Tariff Impact on Country Comparisons

The Section 301 tariffs (25% on Chinese goods) have widened the Vietnam-Indonesia cost gap to approximately 15-20% in China's favor for finished ribbon products (HS 5806.32). For buyers who can absorb tariff costs, China remains USD 0.01-0.04/m cheaper on most styles. For tariff-sensitive buyers prioritizing rules-of-origin, Vietnam and Indonesia factories have become more competitive in 2026.

5. Recommendations by Order Profile

  • Under 2,000m / single style: China or India — lower setup costs, faster
  • 2,000–10,000m: China preferred for full supply chain reliability
  • 10,000m+ / multi-SKU: China (bulk discount) or Vietnam (for US buyers seeking origin diversification)
  • EU market (strict origin rules): Vietnam or domestic EU production

6. How to Use This Benchmark

Request formal quotations from at least two factories in different countries. Use these figures as a sanity-check baseline. Watch for red flags: quotes more than 30% below this range typically indicate substandard quality or missing cost components.

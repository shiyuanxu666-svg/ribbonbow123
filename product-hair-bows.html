Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Are these hair bows safe for babies?

Yes. All our hair bows use OEKO-TEX certified materials. All clips and metal parts are tested for sharp edges and safety. We recommend adult supervision for children under 3 years. Baby headbands use soft, stretchy elastic bands designed specifically for infant comfort.

Q: What is the minimum order quantity?

Stock style/color combinations: MOQ 200 pieces per style. Custom designs (your colors, patterns, or packaging): MOQ 500 pieces per design. Sample orders of 10-30 pieces available for quality assessment at standard wholesale pricing.

Q: Can I get custom hair bow designs with our brand logo?

Absolutely. We offer custom-printed grosgrain ribbon with your logo or pattern for hair bows — MOQ 500m of printed ribbon (approximately 2,000-3,000 standard-sized bows). Lead time: 20-30 days from design approval.

Q: Do you offer private labeling?

Yes. We offer private label options: custom hang tags with your brand logo, branded packaging, and care labels. Private label MOQ: 500 pieces per order.

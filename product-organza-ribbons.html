Types of Organza Ribbons We Offer

Sheer Organza Ribbon

The classic organza — thin, sheer, and crisp with a subtle shimmer. The most popular organza type for gift wrapping, wedding décor, and floral arrangements. Available in 100+ colors from pastels to deep jewel tones.

Double-Sided Organza Ribbon

Sheer on both sides with a slightly more substantial feel than single-sided organza. Ideal for bows and applications where both sides are visible — premium gift wrapping, wedding favors, and formal occasions.

Wired Organza Ribbon

Organza with a thin wire edge running along both sides of the ribbon. This allows the ribbon to be shaped, curled, and formed into decorative shapes without creasing. The #1 choice for wedding florals, corsages, boutonnieres, and craft applications.

Metallic Edge Organza

Organza ribbon with metallic gold or silver wire edges. The metallic trim creates a stunning border effect and is the #1 bestseller for Christmas ribbons, New Year decorations, and premium gift packaging.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is organza ribbon and how is it different from satin?

Organza is a thin, sheer, crisp fabric — typically made from polyester or nylon — with an open weave that is semi-transparent. Unlike satin which is dense and glossy on both sides, organza is lightweight and translucent with a subtle shimmer. Organza is preferred for elegant, decorative, and wedding applications where a light, airy look is desired.

Q: What is wired organza ribbon?

Wired organza has a thin wire edge running along both sides of the ribbon, allowing it to be shaped and curved for floral arrangements, bows, and decorative displays. The wire keeps the ribbon's form without creasing. It is the #1 choice for wedding décor, corsages, and craft applications.

Q: Can organza ribbons be printed on?

Yes. Custom printing on organza is available — digital printing and heat transfer work best on the sheer surface. Screen printing is also available for simple logo applications. MOQ 1,000m for custom printed organza.

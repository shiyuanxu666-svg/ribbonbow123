💡 US Tariff Solution — Vietnam Factory Supply
Some Chinese ribbon products face high US tariffs (ADD ~289% + Section 301 25% = ~314% total for certain HS codes). Smith Ribbon's strategic Vietnam factory can supply PP ribbon, narrow fabric ribbons, and gift wrapping paper from Vietnam origin — reducing US import duty to approximately 6–7.5%. Contact us at the booth for a tariff optimization assessment for your product line.

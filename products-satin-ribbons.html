Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is the difference between satin ribbon and grosgrain ribbon?

A: Satin ribbon has a smooth, glossy face with a luxurious sheen ideal for decorative purposes. Grosgrain ribbon has a ridged, matte texture and is more durable — better suited for structural uses like ribbon bookmarks or strap-like applications.

Q: Can I get a sample before placing a bulk order?

A: Yes. We offer complimentary color swatches (up to 10 colors) for qualified buyers. Custom color samples require a small fee, which is credited against your first bulk order.

Q: What widths are best for wedding bouquet wrapping?

A: 25mm and 38mm are the most popular widths for bouquet wrapping. 38mm provides a more pronounced bow, while 25mm creates an elegant, delicate finish.

Q: Do you offer pre-cut lengths or factory rolls only?

A: Stock items ship as factory rolls (25 or 50 yards). For pre-cut lengths (e.g., 2-inch pieces bagged individually), we offer custom converting services with an MOQ of 5,000 pieces.

Q: What is your production lead time for custom colors?

A: Custom color production typically takes 10–15 days after color approval. Shipping by sea freight takes 15–30 days depending on destination; air freight is available for urgent orders.
